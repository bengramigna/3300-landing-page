# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement
Ben Gramigna is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2026, with a minor in Sports and Recreation Management. He is seeking entry-level roles and internships across business-related fields, including analytics, finance, and general business positions.

The problem this product solves is the limitation of a traditional resume. A resume cannot fully showcase Ben’s personality, depth of experience, or detailed project work. This landing page will allow him to present a more complete professional story, highlight key projects in greater depth, and better communicate his business mindset, leadership experience, and eagerness to learn.

## 02 — Target Audience
The target audience includes business-oriented employers across multiple fields, including analytics, finance, and general business roles. Primary locations include Chicago and surrounding suburbs, with openness to Florida and Boston.

Visitors are looking to quickly understand Ben’s business mindset, leadership experience, willingness to learn, and technical capabilities.

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Increase visibility to employers | 25+ site visitors (30–60 days) |
| Generate recruiter interest | 10 recruiter messages |
| Support job search activity | 10 interviews/applications |
| Expand professional network | 8 new LinkedIn connections |
